LIBRARY

The library is the most important supporting facility in the teaching and learning process. In addition to the library at the faculty level, each study program or BKK within the FKIP environment also has a library to make it easier for the academic community to obtain books, journals or other scientific works.

MULTIMEDIA STUDIO

Multimedia Studio is one of the facilities that is quite important to support and increase the creativity of students, not least for students of Informatics and Computer Engineering Education.

LABORATORY

PTIK Computer Laboratory is a campus facility with the main function of providing practical services for subjects that require the use of computers.
